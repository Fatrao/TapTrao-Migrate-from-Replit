# TapTrao

**Pre-shipment import compliance for SME commodity importers.**

TapTrao helps small and medium importers understand exactly what compliance requirements, documents, and risks apply to a shipment — before money is committed. Once a shipment is active, the TapTrao Shield pack manages supplier document requests, LC cross-checks, a hashed audit trail, and continuous risk monitoring through to docking and release.

🌐 [taptrao.com](https://taptrao.com)

---

## What TapTrao does

**Pre-Ship Requirements**
Know what documents, permits, and compliance steps are required before purchasing or committing to a shipment. Covers EUDR, CBAM, sanctions, phytosanitary requirements, and rules of origin.

**Supplier Ask**
Generate ready-made supplier document request communications. Chase missing certificates, invoices, packing lists, and certificates of origin without manual drafting.

**Document Vault + LC Check**
Upload all shipment documents in one place. Cross-check Letters of Credit against shipment documents to catch discrepancies before bank submission.

**Twinlog**
Every action, upload, and decision recorded with hashing for integrity evidence. A complete, audit-ready trail from first check to release.

**Risk-to-Release Monitor**
Continuous risk score and monetary exposure — duty, demurrage, EUDR, CBAM — tracked and updated until the shipment docks and clears.

## Regulatory coverage

- EUDR (EU Deforestation Regulation) — applicable from 30 Dec 2026 (large/medium operators) and 30 Jun 2027 (small operators)
- CBAM (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) — operational from 1 Jan 2026
- Sanctions and denied party screening
- Phytosanitary and SPS requirements
- Rules of origin
- LC document validation (UCP 600 / ISBP aligned)
